what we do

Private Events

At Nicole Plant Events we specialise in the planning of your tailor-made day. No idea is too grand and no event too small; here at Nicole Plant we make sure that, no matter the event, your day is planned to perfection.

From Birthdays to Anniversaries, Retirement Parties to Engagements, we live to turn your vision in to a reality.

Corporate Functions

Whether it's an essential board meeting or large exhibition, product launch or educational seminar, we have the expertise and experience to make certain that your clients will have the best possible meeting experience.

We work with only select panels of audio-visual, catering and event hire suppliers in order to deliver the ultimate bespoke professional meeting package.

who we are

People

Our team at Nicole Plant Events has over 45 years combined experience of planning bespoke events. This experience means you can rest assured your event is in the safest possible hands, managed by people who are career event planning professionals.

Pride

Planning your event is only part of what we do. We also take great pride in delivering the most professional service, from enquiry, through the planning and delivery stages to the final post-event reflection. It's your event - you want to know that the people organising it truly care about what they do and what you need. At Nicole Plant events, what we do and how we do it matters to us.

Perfection

An event that's well managed is what all of our clients expect.
What we deliver is an event that is precisely tailored to your exact needs, with no small detail left unchekced.

Our experience tells us that striving for perfection when planning your event leaves your guests feeling that they have experienced something different, an event that delivers not only in the key areas, but that also considers all the little things that really make the difference.
